History of Portal

The story of PORTAL

Traditionally in Government Polytechnic Pune Assignment Submission was done manually. Students write the assignment on Assignment sheets & Attach print outs of the practical performed in the labs.However, this system is very much confined to receiving the assignments. In this case, the instructor is left with the formidable task of asking for Students Journals, reading, evaluating and marking them, employing the conventional process. For the resolution of this problem, a special system ought to be devised in order to carry out the bulk of the marking process and verification of the assignments. Such a system would have to be capable of functioning through the college network. The same system would have also to be connected with available technological tools helping in the process of marking assignments.
